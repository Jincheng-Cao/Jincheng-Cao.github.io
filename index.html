Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of ECE at UT Austin, advised by Prof. Aryan Mokhtari. Previously, I received a B.S. degree in Mathematics with high honors from UT as well and spent a year in graduate program of Operations Research and Industrial Engineering.

My current research focuses on the theory and applications of convex and non-convex optimization in large-scale machine learning and data science problems. Furthermore, I am interested in exploring the area of bilevel optimization and federated learning in near future.
