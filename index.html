Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Department of Electrical and Computer Engineerin at The University of Texas at Austin, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Aryan Mokhtari. I am also a part of the Wireless Networking and Communications Group. Previously, I received a B.S. degree in Mathematics with high honors from UT as well and spent a year in the graduate program of Operations Research and Industrial Engineering. During this period, I worked with Prof. Stephen G. Walker and Prof. John J. Hasenbein.

My research interests generally lie in the intersection of optimization, statistics and machine learning. My current research focuses on bilevel optimization, large-scale stochastic optimization, and their applications in machine learning.
